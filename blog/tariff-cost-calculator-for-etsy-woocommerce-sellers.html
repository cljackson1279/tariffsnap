Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) for Etsy & WooCommerce Sellers

How do I handle customs fees for my Etsy customers?

Etsy's policies state that buyers are responsible for paying any customs fees. However, unexpected fees can lead to unhappy customers. By calculating your landed cost, you can choose to incorporate some or all of the tariff cost into your retail price for a better customer experience.

Are there any WooCommerce plugins for calculating tariffs?

Yes, there are several plugins available for WooCommerce that can help you calculate and charge for duties and taxes at checkout. However, these are primarily for charging your customers. TariffSnap helps you, the seller, understand your own costs when importing products.

What if I source materials from multiple countries for my handmade Etsy products?

This can be complex. The tariff rate is typically based on the country of origin of the final product. If you are significantly transforming materials from multiple countries, you may need to consult with a customs broker to determine the correct country of origin for your products.

How can I reduce my tariff costs?

One of the best ways to reduce tariff costs is to source from countries with lower tariff rates. Your TariffSnap report can help you identify which of your products are most affected by tariffs, so you can explore alternative sourcing options.
