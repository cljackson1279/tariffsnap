Manual Calculation Formula:

Landed Cost = Product Cost + Shipping + (Product Cost √ó Tariff Rate)

Example:

  • Product cost from China: $10
  • Shipping: $2
  • China tariff rate: 30%

  • Total landed cost: $10 + $2 + $3 = $15
